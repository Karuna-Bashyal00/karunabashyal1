Nepal US Relations
Diplomatic Relations Nepal and the United States of America (USA) established diplomatic 
relations on April 25,1947. The US is the second country, only after the UK, with which 
Nepal established diplomatic relations. Nepal opened up its Embassy in Washington D.C. 
on February 03, 1958. On August 06, 1959, American Embassy in Kathmandu was opened.
Exchange of Visits From USA to Nepal High level visits have served to further strengthen
the bilateral relations between Nepal and the UnitedStates of America. Prominent visits to
Nepal from the US include the visits of US First Lady Hillary Clintonin 1995;Assistant
Secretary of State Christina Rocca in 2001, 2002, 2003 and 2005; Secretary of State Colin
Powell in January 2002; Richard Boucher, AssistantSecretary of State in May 2006, Nove
