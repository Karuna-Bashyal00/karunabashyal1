Relinquishment of Nepali Citizenship

Nepali citizens applying for the Relinquishment of Nepali Citizenship are required to submit the supporting documents mentioned below.

* Duly filled in application form (Download Form HERE)

* Original Nepali Citizenship Card.

* One recent photograph.

* Copy of the U.S. Passport.

* Copy of the U.S. Certificate of Naturalization.

* Self-addressed prepaid return envelope with tracking number. (Not Required for Self Pickup)
