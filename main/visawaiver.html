Visa Waiver/Change of Status Application

Duly fill out the application FORM for Visa Waiver/Change of Status.

Documents required for Visa Waiver Certificate:

1. Copies of DS-2019 form.

2. Case file number obtained after completing online DS-3035. (Instructions)

3. Passport.

4. U.S. visa and a copy of job termination letter/notice (if applicable).

5. Pre-paid Return Envelope for sending a copy of ‘No Objection Statement’ back to your address.
