Visas

Embassy of Nepal issues Diplomatic, Official and Tourist Visas according to the Immigration Rules and Regulation of Nepal. The applicant must fill in the information requested in the application form. It is the right of the Government of Nepal to grant or not to grant visa to any foreigner. Visa is good for 6 Months from the date of issue.

Nationals of the following countries are Mandatorily required to obtain a tourist visa from the Embassy prior to their departure from the United States to Nepal: Nigeria, Ghana, Zimbabwe, Swaziland, Cameroon, Somalia, Liberia, Ethiopia, Iraq, Palestine, Afghanistan, Syria.

Refugees with travel documents are also not eligible for an on-arrival visa in Nepal, so they should also obtain a visa from the Embassy prior to their departure from the United States to Nepal.
